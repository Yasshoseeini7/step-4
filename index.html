About Marivan

Marivan is a beautiful city located in the west of Iran, known for its stunning landscapes and rich culture. The city is surrounded by mountains and is famous for its lakes and natural beauty.

Culture

The people of Marivan primarily speak Kurdish, and the city is known for its vibrant traditions, music, and dance. Kurdish culture is rich in history and is celebrated through various festivals and events.
